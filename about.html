About Us

United Gears was established in 1988 by S. Sher Singh Sokhi. Since then we are manufacturing shafts, gears, bushes and other kinds of spare parts for agriculture machinery. Our Chairman S. Sher Singh has been working in this industry for 40 years now. With their invaluable experience in hand and the modern day manufacturing techniques we are able to manufacture high quality precise parts for the agriculture machinery. We mainly deals in the manufacturing of Shafts and Bushes of various agriculture machinery. Our products include spare parts of Rotavator, Straw reaper, Harambha thresher, and many more.
