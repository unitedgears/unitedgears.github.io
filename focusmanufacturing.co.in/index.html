Our Services

We do all kinds of job work including On-demand Manufacturing, Product development and Export.

Manufacturing

We are specialised in all kind of job work including Pattern making, Match paltes, Core shooter dies, Cold box dies, Shell Moulding, Hand mould pattern and many more. With over 36 years of experience and latest machining technologies we are able to provide you a quality which is tough to compete with.

Product Development

We do not brag about our witty design techniques and acumen but ask any of our clients and they will tell you how much production time and efforts have been saved just because of an intelligent design which is optimised for production rate.

Export

We manufacture on demand products and export them as well. Our manafacture capabilities ranges from mechanical goods like Automobile parts and Irrigation parts to synthetic products like seals and plastic mouldings. Currently we are exporting products like Aluminium custom couplings, D.I. Caps, Irrigation Tee, etc.
