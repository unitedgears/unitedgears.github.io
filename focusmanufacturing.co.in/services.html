Our Services

We do all kinds of job work including On-demand Manufacturing, Product development and Export.

We are specialised in all kind of job work including Pattern making, Match paltes, Core shooter dies, Cold box dies, Shell Moulding, Hand mould pattern and many more.We manufacture on demand products and export them as well. Our manafacture capabilities ranges from mechanical goods like Automobile parts and Irrigation parts to synthetic products like seals and plastic mouldings. Currently we are exporting products like Aluminium custom couplings, D.I. Caps, Irrigation Tee, etc.

Manufacturing

We are specialized in manufacturing of mechanical components. Our manufacturing facility include high class VMC machines and american casting furnaces. We deal in production products like Aluminium Custom Couplings, D.I Cap, Irrigation Tee etc.

Product Development

We have developed products for some the really prestigious companies and we are well known our intelligent design. We have some of the best engineers who works together in a team to make some great products.

Export

We do export on demand products to Australia and Dubai and we are open to export in other countries as well. Currently we are exporting Aluminium Custom Couplings, D.I Caps and Irrigation Tee.
